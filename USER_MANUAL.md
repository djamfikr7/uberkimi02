## üöÄ Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Web Browser:**
- Google Chrome (latest version)
- Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Microsoft Edge (latest version)

**Mobile Devices:**
- iOS 12.0 or later
- Android 6.0 or later

### Installation

#### Web Application
1. Open your preferred web browser
2. Navigate to the Uber Clone application URL
3. No installation required - the application runs directly in your browser

#### Mobile Application
1. Open your device's app store
2. Search for "Uber Clone"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Wait for the installation to complete
5. Tap "Open" to launch the application

### Account Creation

#### Rider Registration
1. Open the Uber Clone application
2. Tap "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols)
5. Enter your first and last name
6. Provide your phone number
7. Tap "Register"
8. Check your email for a verification link
9. Click the verification link to activate your account

#### Driver Registration
1. Open the Uber Clone Driver application
2. Tap "Become a Driver"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password
5. Enter your personal information
6. Provide your driver's license information
7. Enter your vehicle details
8. Upload required documents
9. Submit your application
10. Wait for approval from administrators

#### Administrator Access
Administrator accounts are created by system administrators and cannot be self-registered.

## üöï Rider Guide

### Requesting a Ride

1. **Open the Application**
   - Launch the Uber Clone app on your device
   - Sign in with your credentials

2. **Set Pickup Location**
   - The app will automatically detect your current location
   - To change pickup location:
     - Tap the pickup field
     - Enter an address or landmark
     - Select from suggested locations

3. **Set Destination**
   - Tap the destination field
   - Enter your destination address
   - Select from suggested locations
   - Save frequently visited locations as favorites

4. **Select Vehicle Type**
   - Choose from available vehicle options:
     - **UberX**: Affordable everyday rides
     - **Comfort**: Extra legroom and newer cars
     - **Black**: Premium rides with luxury vehicles
     - **Pool**: Shared rides to reduce costs

5. **Review Ride Details**
   - Check pickup and destination addresses
   - Confirm estimated fare
   - Review estimated arrival time

6. **Request Ride**
   - Tap "Confirm Ride" or "Request Now"
   - Wait for driver acceptance

### During Your Ride

1. **Track Your Driver**
   - View driver's name, photo, and vehicle information
   - See real-time location on the map
   - Estimated time of arrival (ETA) updates automatically

2. **Contact Your Driver**
   - Use the in-app chat to communicate
   - Call driver directly if needed
   - Share your ride details with friends or family

3. **Safety Features**
   - Emergency contact sharing
   - Ride verification code
   - Anonymous phone numbers
   - 24/7 support assistance

### Completing Your Ride

1. **Arrival Confirmation**
   - Driver will confirm arrival at pickup location
   - Verify driver's identity and vehicle details

2. **During the Ride**
   - Track progress on the map
   - Communicate with driver through chat if needed

3. **Ride Completion**
   - Driver will end the ride
   - Review the final fare
   - Rate your driver experience (1-5 stars)
   - Provide written feedback (optional)

### Payment Methods

1. **Adding Payment Methods**
   - Go to "Payment" in the menu
   - Tap "Add Payment Method"
   - Choose from:
     - Credit/Debit Card
     - PayPal
     - Apple Pay/Google Pay
     - Cash (where available)

2. **Managing Payments**
   - Set default payment method
   - Remove expired cards
   - Update billing information

### Trip History

1. **View Past Trips**
   - Tap "Your Trips" in the menu
   - See list of completed rides
   - Filter by date range

2. **Trip Details**
   - View pickup and destination addresses
   - See exact fare breakdown
   - Download receipts
   - Re-book similar trips

### Settings and Preferences

1. **Account Settings**
   - Update personal information
   - Change password
   - Manage email and SMS notifications

2. **Favorites**
   - Save frequently visited locations
   - Set home and work addresses
   - Quick access to favorite destinations

3. **Accessibility**
   - Adjust text size
   - Enable voice announcements
   - Wheelchair accessibility options

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Unable to sign in
**Solutions**:
- Check email and password spelling
- Reset password using "Forgot Password"
- Verify account through email
- Contact support if issues persist

#### Location Services
**Issue**: App can't detect location
**Solutions**:
- Enable location services in device settings
- Grant location permissions to the app
- Restart the application
- Check GPS signal strength

#### Payment Issues
**Issue**: Payment method declined
**Solutions**:
- Verify card details are correct
- Check card expiration date
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try alternative payment method

#### Ride Request Problems
**Issue**: No drivers available
**Solutions**:
- Try different pickup location
- Adjust pickup time
- Select different vehicle type
- Expand service area radius

## ‚ùì FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Go to your profile settings and update your information. Changes are saved automatically.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

**Q: Can I schedule rides in advance?**
A: Yes, you can schedule rides up to 30 days in advance through the app.

**Q: What happens if I need to cancel a ride?**
A: You can cancel rides free of charge within the first few minutes. After that, cancellation fees may apply.

### Rider Questions

**Q: How is the fare calculated?**
A: Fares are based on distance, time, and demand. The estimated fare is shown before you request the ride.

**Q: Can I split the fare with friends?**
A: Yes, you can split fares with up to 4 people using the in-app split fare feature.

**Q: How do I rate my driver?**
A: After completing your ride, you'll be prompted to rate your driver on a 1-5 star scale.

**Q: What safety features are available?**
A: We offer emergency contact sharing, ride verification codes, anonymous phone numbers, and 24/7 support.

### Driver Questions

**Q: How much can I earn?**
A: Earnings vary based on location, time, and demand. The app provides earning estimates.

**Q: What are the requirements to drive?**
A: You must have a valid driver's license, clean driving record, and insured vehicle that meets our standards.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Payments are deposited weekly via direct deposit. Instant payouts are available for a small fee.

**Q: What happens if I get a bad rating?**
A: Consistently low ratings may affect your ability to receive ride requests. We provide feedback to help improve.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: Our app works on iOS and Android smartphones and tablets, plus web browsers on desktop computers.

**Q: How much data does the app use?**
A: The app uses approximately 2-5 MB per ride for maps and communications.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Basic functions require internet connectivity, but you can view saved locations offline.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: Updates are available through your device's app store. We recommend keeping the app updated.
